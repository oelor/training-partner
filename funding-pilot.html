Funding research alpha

A Milo-operated funding research queue, separate from the nonprofit surface.

This is one narrow revenue surface inside Training Partner. It is independent from The Mat Association. Requests are screened by Milo and only accepted when the queue, the fit, and the current system state make the work credible.

What accepted requests may include
