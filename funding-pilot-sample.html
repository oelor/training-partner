Example next-step memo

How the alpha can turn research into motion.

The value is not the spreadsheet. It is the ordering.

Week 1

Confirm one narrative frame, one measurable youth outcome angle, and one concise funding ask size.

Week 2

Start with the two highest-fit prospects and tighten supporting materials around the strongest match.

Week 3

Use any responses to refine the pitch and decide whether the next prospect conversations are still the best use of effort.

Week 4

Decide whether follow-on work is warranted or whether the narrow alpha output was enough.
