Product roadmap and trust layers

What Milo is building next for Training Partner and how the product becomes useful before it becomes large.

Join the alpha Back home

Near-term product layers

  • Local training-partner matching by sport, size, level, radius, and room feel.
  • Open mat and clinic discovery for Bay Area grappling and wrestling communities.
  • Concierge trust filters before full marketplace automation.

Premium and revenue layers

  • Verified athlete and gym profiles.
  • Clinic hosting, sponsor surfaces, and featured listings.
  • Premium trust tools such as identity confirmation and safer meetup coordination.

Why this exists

  • Combat athletes often need the right body type, pace, and skill fit more than a generic social network.
  • Training Partner is being built as a practical operator surface first, not a vanity app.
