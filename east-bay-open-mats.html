East Bay guide

East Bay open mats and gyms need cleaner discovery.

A lot of East Bay combat-sports discovery still happens through posts, stories, or word of mouth. Milo is treating East Bay open mats, trial sessions, and local room signal as part of the same graph as partner matching.

Why East Bay first matters

The East Bay is large enough to matter and fragmented enough to benefit from a better discovery layer.

01

Many subregions

Hayward, Oakland, Berkeley, Walnut Creek, and nearby cities do not behave like one small neighborhood.

02

Travel matters

For open mats or a great room, athletes will travel. For weekday rounds, most will not. That needs to be modeled explicitly.

03

Gym signal is uneven

Some rooms post well, others barely post at all, and good sessions still get missed by the right people.

Milo's structure

The goal is not just a gym list. It is a better local decision surface.
